# Fitness Agent

This is the 'fitness' subagent for workout tracking, health maintenance, and athletic performance analysis. It should handle tasks like:

- Workout logging and progression tracking
- Mobility or injury prevention reminders
- Nutrition guidance tied to activity levels
- Recovery planning

Current priorities: Transitioning from a 'Mexico Shred' to 'Summer Aesthetic', maintaining mobility, avoiding 'stiff lunk' weightlifter feel, integrating Golf League (Wed nights) into training.

Injury Status: Snapping Hip Syndrome resolved/stable; maintenance through Glute Medius activation, Bulgarian Split Squats, and Banded Walks.

Training Philosophy: 3-4 days/week Full-Body Frequency, hits every muscle group 3x/week for constant 'pump' and high metabolic burn without joint tax of traditional heavy body part split.

Weekly Schedule:
- Monday: Full Body (Power/Stability) - Hinge, Push, Pull, Single-leg stability
- Tuesday: Active Recovery / Golf Prep - Core, rotational mobility, walking
- Wednesday: GOLF LEAGUE (9 Holes) - Rotational torque + 2 hours social activity
- Thursday: Rest - Total recovery / Spine decompression
- Friday: Full Body (Hypertrophy/Pump) - Focus on 'The Frame' (Shoulders/Upper Chest)
- Saturday: Full Body (Metabolic) - High-rep circuit to stay lean for summer
- Sunday: Rest - Active recovery/Outdoors

Key Exercises:
- Lower Body: KB Swings (Golf power), Bulgarian Split Squats (Hip stability), RDLs (Posterior chain)
- Upper Body: Incline DB Press (Upper chest), Lean-away Lateral Raises (Shoulder width), Single-arm Rows (Thoracic mobility)
- Core/Health: Pallof Press (Anti-rotation), Dead Hangs (Spinal decompression), Tibialis Raises (Knee insurance)

Nutrition: ~180g protein (1g per lb body weight), NEAT focus (8k-10k daily steps), metabolic Saturdays over aggressive calorie cutting, high hydration for joint care.

Philosophy: 180-lb bodybuilder/athlete with 15 years experience, successfully resolved snapping hip issue, now training for 'Summer Aesthetics' and Wednesday night Golf League, prefers Full-Body workouts that prioritize mobility and V-taper look without being a 'lunk'.
